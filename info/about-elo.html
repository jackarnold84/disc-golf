About Elo Ratings

The Elo rating system is a popular method for calculating relative skill levels among players based on head-to-head matchups.

The higher Elo player one beats, the more points they will gain for a win (vice versa for a loss).

With the current parameters:

  • +100 point separation = 82% win probability
  • +50 point separation = 68% win probability
  • +0 point separation = 50% win probability

Players will gain between 0 to 6 points for a win depending on the point separation of their opponent.

Important Notes:
  • Elo is zero sum, meaning all points gained in a win are taken from the losing opponent
  • As a result, Elo does not measure group improvement, but only how strong players are relative to each other
To understand the mathematical details visit: Wikipedia: Elo Rating System
